================================================================================
  JERRY — START HERE
================================================================================

YOUR ROLE: CEO / owner. You use the app. The computer team handles the rest.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OPEN THE APP (every day)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Double-click the **LJC Accounting** icon on your desktop or taskbar
     — OR double-click **START-APP.bat** in your project folder

  2. Your browser opens to:  https://ljc-accounting-app.onrender.com

  3. Log in with your usual email and password

  4. Banking → Reconcile → pick account → Start reconciling

  Updates appear on the website automatically. You do not need to rebuild
  anything on your PC.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIRST TIME (or if shortcuts still open localhost)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. In your project folder, double-click:  INSTALL-SHORTCUTS.bat

  2. That replaces desktop and taskbar shortcuts with the web version.

  3. If the old icon stays on the taskbar, right-click it → Unpin, then
     right-click the new desktop icon → Pin to taskbar.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RECONCILE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Left side = bank statement  |  Right side = your register
  • Click "Import bank statement" to load OFX or PDF
  • Check off cleared lines until Difference = $0.00
  • Then click Reconcile Now

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHEN SOMETHING BREAKS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Tell your AI assistant in Cursor what you see (screenshot helps).
  You do not need to run commands — that is what the project rule is for.
